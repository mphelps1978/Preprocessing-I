Jean Luc Picard

Introduction

My name is Jean-Luc Picard. I have served the majority of my years as an officer in Starfleet, where I obtained the rank of Admiral. I am seeking employment in the field of archaeology, where my passions can be put to good use.

Skillsets

I bring a wide variety of skills along with me. I am a leader first an foremost. My time at Starfleet has given me the opportunity to command vessels with as many as 6,000 souls on board. With that privelidge came a great deal of responsibility. In addition, my time in Starfleet has allowed me to amass the historical and archaeological heritage of countless worlds and civilizations

Work History

  • 2372: USS Enterprise E: Commanding Officer
